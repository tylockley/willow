Empathetic Economics: Homelessness and Art in Alaska

2018

Written for a college rhetoric class, this essay examines the homelessness situation in Anchorage, Alaska. It examines the systematic causes and consequences and explores how art has been used in the community to raise awareness and compassion.

Gender Differences in Literature: By Choice or Force?

2017

Written for a college linguistics class, this essay analyzes the dialectical differences between four American literary classics, finding that culture influences language while language is reciprocal of culture.
